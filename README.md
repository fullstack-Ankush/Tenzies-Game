The Grid: You start with a grid of 10 individual, randomly rolled dice.

Freeze Dice: Click on any die to "freeze" or lock its current value. Frozen dice change color and won't change on subsequent rolls.

Roll: Click the Roll button to change the numbers of all unfrozen dice.

Strategy: Look for the most common number on your initial roll, freeze them, and quickly roll the rest to match them!

Winning: You win when all 10 dice display the exact same value and are successfully frozen. A confetti explosion will celebrate your victory!

🔮 Roadmap (Future Release Features)
Here is a sneak peek at what is planned for future versions of the Tenzies Game:

⏱️ Phase 1: Mechanics & Tracking
Game Timer: A real-time stopwatch to track exactly how many seconds it takes you to win.

Roll Counter: A tracker showing the total number of rolls taken to achieve a full set.

Local Leaderboard: Storage using localStorage to save your personal best high scores (fastest time and fewest rolls).

🎨 Phase 2: Visuals & Experience
Realistic 3D Dice: Upgrading the visual layout from flat numbers to dynamic 3D dice faces (using dots instead of numerals or implementing CSS/Three.js styling).

Retro Theme Toggle: Introducing a specialized monochromatic/pixel-art aesthetic mode for a classic arcade vibe.

⚔️ Phase 3: Advanced Gameplay
Custom Difficulty Modes: Options to scale difficulty (e.g., playing with 12 or 15 dice, or introducing a strict countdown timer mode).

Multiplayer Versus: A real-time competitive split-screen or local pass-and-play mechanic to see who has the fastest fingers.
